Chunking W3.CSS
The chunking strategy is used to reduce the cognative load as you learn new information. Break down information into small, relavant and simple chunks. Put no more than 5-6 bits of information in each category!

1. Break larger amounts of information into smaller units
2.Identify similarities or patterns
3. Organize the information
4. Group information into manageable units

For Example:
1. Phone numbers are not typically seen or remembered as a long string of numbers like 0830523694, but rather 083-052-3694.
2. Birthdays are typically not recalled by 02021996, but rather 02-02-1996.
