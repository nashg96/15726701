A place for everything and everything in its place.

Keep your home organised conistently as it will make it easier for you to remember where everything is:
1. Have a key rack for all your house keys.
2. Group similar types of items together
3. Store items as close as possible to where you will use them
4. Place frequently used items in easy-to-get-to locations
5. When you use something, put it back where it belongs
