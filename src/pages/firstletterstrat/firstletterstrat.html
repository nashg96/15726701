First letter cueing W3.CSS
This is good for recalling someones name. Go through the letters of the alphabet - it helps to prompt recall of the name.

You can also use first letters to create acronyms for something you need to remember.

For example:
PEMDAS is a sequence in solving or evaluating math equations. Parenthesis, Exponent, Multiplication, Division, Addition and Subtraction.

To help remember this sequence of letters in their correct order you could create the sentance "Please Excuse My Dear Aunt Sally" -> PEMDAS.
