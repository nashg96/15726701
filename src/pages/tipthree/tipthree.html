Use a note book W3.CSS
Using a note book can be hugely beneficial if you struggle with your memory. It can be used for anything you need to remember such as:
> A grocery list for when you go shopping
> Taking down someones name if you fear you might forget it later
> Storing contact details or addresses

Getting into the habit of using the notebook every day is highly encouraged. Think of it almost as a second brain.
